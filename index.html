Tools & Process

Learning the Industry’s Tools and Process

ObjectSPY students don’t just learn the theoretical concepts, they learn the detail implementation of how a professional would execute the job at work. Students learn to implement the concept using the industry level applications. Upon graduation of our courses, students can easily blend in with the professionals and immerse themselves in the technical community.

Learn from Scratch. No Prior Experience Needed.

Automation (UFT)

Automating manual work is key in saving time and saving resources. We will start with vbScript, a very easy step by step learning curve to enter the world of automation. Students also get introduced to Selenium with Java (OOP).

Databases (SQL)

How to Query the data? Everywhere you look today, you see data all over; and here at ObjectSPY we teach our students the Structured Query Language to make sense of all these data that plays a huge part in the application.

API (REST / SOAP)

Whether the API is a restful application that uses XML or JSON, or SOAP-based XML container, test scripts should be built to check response time & accuracy. Students learn the fundamentals and becomes a pro step by step.

Development Life Cycle and Documentation

Agile (Kanban / Scrum)

In this industry, there is no more jumping to code and getting to production. To do things efficiently, we start with a better understanding of the Application Lifecycle. Students learn the concept of welcoming & adapting to changes.

Jira / Rally

Managing the rapid changes to the requirements and adapting to the first track environment is not so easy without a good ticketing and documenting system. Students learns the best process and masters the industry level tools.

DevOps

From the requirement gathering to analyzing, developing, testing, tracking defects and deploying – it would be difficult to maintain without a proper portal for storage and traceability. Tools like ALM and TFS are your best bet!

FAQ

You have questions; we have answers

  •  What is the schedule for the classes?

    All our classes are online via webinar, so you can join from anywhere. Classes are every Saturday and Sundays at 9:30AM Central Standard Time (CST). You should block you schedule until 2:30pm CST. Some classes may end early.

  •   How long is the duration of the class?

    Our main goal is to transfer a vast amount of knowledge with the best quality to our students. Depending upon the course, the class length varies. We estimate that our courses will be completed within 4 months, however, due to holidays it can extend.

  •  What is the class environment like?

    The courses are not design like school, college or university. Each of our sessions are recorded and distributed to students. We understand that people have life and things happen, if you cannot join class, you can watch the record sessions and schedule 1:1 with mentors in your own free time! We believe in work-hard and play-hard. Therefore, we give you multiple short breaks during the 4-hour session?

  •  What if I miss a class?

    All classes are recorded. Therefore, if you cannot join class, you can watch the record sessions and schedule 1:1 with mentors in your own free time!

  •   What do I need for the class?

    The only thing that you need is a laptop and a smart phone. We provide our students with all necessary software!
