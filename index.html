Student Milestones

Each chapter consists of various assignment. Students are to complete them for self-evaluationin order to demonstrate their progress.If you are doing the course online, you are to do one-on-one sessions after each chapter. Students work in teams and directly with the instructors to ensure they’ve mastered key concepts before progressing. If students need additional support, they receive additional direct mentorship, and, if necessary, they’ll have the opportunity to repeat the course at no extra cost for the 2nd time around. Upon completion of each chapter, students are to check off the topics from their Student Milestones.
